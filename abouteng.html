About

We are a transport forwarding company that has its own fleet of more than 10 trucks. The young team, thanks to its professionalism and ambitions, cooperates with more than 50 companies from 10 countries of the world. Among such countries are Germany, the Czech Republic, Spain, Belgium, Turkey, Poland, Slovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, Moldova and the Baltic states.

What do we offer?

Our staff, which consists of logisticians and brokers, will be happy to help solve your questions. We have experienced logisticians who will help you plan the best route and quickly pick up the cargo you need. Managers will quickly process your order. Our customs brokers will help in solving issues related to customs, and highly qualified drivers will quickly and safely deliver the cargo you need.
